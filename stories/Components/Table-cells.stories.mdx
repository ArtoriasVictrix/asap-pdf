- `cell-p-N`
- `cell-px-N`
- `cell-py-N`

## Vertical alignment

Vertical alignment of content in the table cells is set with the help of classes:

- `cells-va-bl` (baseline)
- `cells-va-t` (top)
- `cells-va-m` (middle)
- `cells-va-b` (bottom)
- `cells-va-tb` (text-bottom)
- `cells-va-tt` (text-top)

**Example:**

<Canvas>
<table class="table table--borders table--outer-border table--borders-black cells-va-t">
	<thead>
		<tr class="cells-va-m">
			<th class="h-44">Month</th>
			<th class="cells-va-b">Expenses</th>
			<th>Income</th>
		</tr>
	</thead>
	<tbody>
		<tr>
			<td class="text-lg h-44">January</td>
			<td class="cells-va-m">10 000</td>
			<td class="text-xl">15 000</td>
		</tr>
	</tbody>
</table>
</Canvas>
